# Boat/Watercraft Insurance

Owning a boat or other type of watercraft is a thrill, but also comes with the need to provide specific insurance protection. In most cases, those who own a motorboat or similar type of craft are required by law to carry a certain amount of liability insurance. These laws can vary from state to state, so make sure to consult with our expert agents at KLM Insurance Solutions Inc. to find out what the requirements are in your area.
