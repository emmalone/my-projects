## Protect Your Personal Property

Your landlord's insurance policy covers the building structure, but your furniture, electronics, clothing, jewelry, and other personal belongings are your responsibility to insure. Renters insurance provides financial protection if your possessions are damaged or stolen due to fire, theft, vandalism, water damage, or other covered events.

The cost of replacing everything you own can add up quickly. Think about your television, computer, furniture, clothes, kitchen items, and more. Without renters insurance, you'd have to pay out of pocket to replace these items. Our affordable policies give you peace of mind knowing you're protected.

## More Than Just Property Protection

Renters insurance offers valuable coverage beyond just your belongings:

- Personal liability protection if someone is injured in your rental
- Medical payments to others for minor injuries
- Additional living expenses if you're displaced from your home
- Legal defense costs if you're sued for covered claims
- Worldwide coverage for your personal property

This comprehensive protection typically costs less than a dollar per day, making it one of the most affordable insurance options available. The small investment can save you thousands if the unexpected happens.
