# Boat Insurance

## Overview

Watercraft insurance for personal recreational vessels.

## Coverage Types

- Hull coverage
- Liability
- Medical payments
- Uninsured boater
- Personal effects

## Market Niche

Boat insurance represents a specialty market with good margins and loyal clients.

## Considerations

- Seasonal policies
- Navigation limits
- Agreed value vs market value
- Storage and winterization
