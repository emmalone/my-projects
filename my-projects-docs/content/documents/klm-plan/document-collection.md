## Overview

## Implementation

### Phase 1: Discovery
Client completes initial checklist identifying which documents exist and where they are located.

### Phase 2: Upload
Documents uploaded to Digital Life Vault with appropriate categorization.

### Phase 3: Organization
System creates a master index with location information for documents not stored digitally.

### Phase 4: Sharing
Critical documents shared with trusted family members or advisors with appropriate permissions.

### Phase 5: Maintenance
Annual review ensures documents are current and accessible.

## Features

- **Checklist Progress**: Visual indicator of completion
- **Location Tracking**: Physical location of documents not uploaded
- **Expiration Alerts**: Reminders when documents need updating
- **Gap Identification**: Highlights missing critical documents
- **Templates**: Sample documents and explanations
- **Guidance**: Why each document matters and how to obtain

## Agent Value

Provides structure for client meetings and demonstrates thoroughness. Creates natural upsell opportunities (identify missing coverages, estate planning needs, etc).
