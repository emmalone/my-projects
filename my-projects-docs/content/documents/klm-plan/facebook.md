# Facebook Marketing Strategy

## Overview

Facebook advertising and organic presence for lead generation and brand building.

## Target Audiences

### Saved Audiences
1. **Homeowners 35-55** - Personal lines bundle prospects
2. **Small Business Owners** - Commercial lines prospects
3. **Life Insurance Shoppers 45-65** - Life & legacy platform
4. **Parents of Teens** - Auto insurance (new drivers)
5. **Retirees 60-75** - Final expense, Medicare supplement

### Custom Audiences
- Website visitors (retargeting)
- Customer email list (lookalikes)
- Engaged video viewers

## Campaign Types

### Lead Generation
- Direct quote requests
- Download guides/checklists
- Free insurance review offers
- Life & legacy vault trial

### Brand Awareness
- Educational content
- Client testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes agent stories
- Community involvement

### Retargeting
- Quote abandonment
- Website visitors who didn't convert
- Email list re-engagement

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Posting Schedule
- **Monday**: Insurance tip or FAQ
- **Wednesday**: Client story or testimonial
- **Friday**: Community involvement or team highlight

### Monthly Themes
- January: New year planning
- February: Home safety (winter)
- March: Spring cleaning (policy review)
- April: Tax season (life insurance & estate planning)
- May: Home buying season
- June: Summer safety (boat, travel)
- July: Vacation coverage reminders
- August: Back to school (teen drivers)
- September: Life insurance awareness month
- October: Small business month
- November: Holiday safety
- December: Year-end planning

## Ad Creative Guidelines

### Visuals
- Local imagery when possible
- Diverse family representations
- Professional but approachable
- Avoid stock insurance cliches

### Copy
- Lead with benefit, not product
- Use customer language, not industry jargon
- Clear call-to-action
- Mobile-friendly (short paragraphs)

### Video
- 15-30 seconds ideal
- Captions required (sound-off viewing)
- Strong first 3 seconds
- End with clear CTA

## Budget

- **Testing Phase**: $500/month minimum
- **Scaling**: $2,000-5,000/month based on ROI
- Allocate 20% to testing new audiences/creative

## Key Metrics

- Cost per lead (CPL) target: $15-25 for personal lines, $50-100 for commercial
- Click-through rate (CTR): 1-3% benchmark
- Relevance score: 7+ goal
- Lead quality score (agent feedback)
- Lead-to-quote conversion: 30%+ goal
- ROI: 3:1 minimum

## A/B Testing Framework

Always test:
- Audience variations
- Image vs video
- Headline variations
- CTA button text
- Landing page design

## Compliance Considerations

- All ads reviewed for insurance regulatory compliance
- Disclaimers where required
- Privacy policy linked
- TCPA compliance for phone leads
- E&O coverage for marketing claims

## Tools

- **Facebook Ads Manager**: Campaign management
- **Meta Pixel**: Conversion tracking
- **Facebook Business Suite**: Organic posting
- **Canva**: Creative design
- **Landing page builder**: LeadPages, Unbounce, or custom
