# Home Insurance

## Overview

Homeowners insurance protecting property and liability.

## Products Offered

- HO3 (Special Form)
- HO5 (Comprehensive)
- HO6 (Condo)
- HO4 (Renters)

## Bundle Strategy

Home + Auto bundling is a key retention and pricing strategy.

## Risk Considerations

- Weather-related exposures
- Age of home and updates
- Security systems and discounts
- Replacement cost vs actual cash value
