# Personal Liability / Umbrella Insurance

## Overview

Excess liability coverage that sits above auto and home policies.

## Typical Limits

- $1M to $5M most common
- $10M+ for high net worth clients

## Target Market

- Clients with significant assets to protect
- Business owners
- Landlords
- Anyone with elevated liability exposure

## Cross-Sell Opportunity

Umbrella policies are excellent retention tools and often indicate clients with other planning needs (estate, life, etc).

## Requirements

- Minimum underlying auto liability
- Minimum underlying home liability
- May require excess uninsured motorist
