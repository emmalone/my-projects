    Comprehensive Protection for Riders#

    Motorcycle insurance is different from standard auto insurance because bikes face distinct risks and require specialized coverage. Our policies protect you from liability, collision damage, theft, and more. We understand that every rider has different needs based on their bike, riding habits, and budget.

    We work with Pennsylvania’s top insurance carriers to find you the best coverage at competitive rates. Whether you’re a weekend warrior or a daily commuter, we’ll help you navigate your options and select the policy that gives you peace of mind on every ride.

    What’s Covered Under Motorcycle Insurance#

    A comprehensive motorcycle insurance policy typically includes several key protections:

    • Liability coverage for bodily injury and property damage
    • Collision coverage for damage to your bike
    • Comprehensive coverage for theft, vandalism, and weather damage
    • Uninsured/underinsured motorist protection
    • Medical payments coverage
    • Custom parts and equipment coverage
    • Roadside assistance and towing

    The level of coverage you need depends on factors like your bike’s value, how often you ride, and Pennsylvania’s legal requirements. Our experienced agents can explain each option and help you build a policy that fits your needs.

    Finding the Right Policy for Your Ride#

    At KLM Insurance Solutions Inc., we take the time to understand your riding habits and what you value most. Some riders prioritize comprehensive coverage for expensive custom bikes, while others need basic liability to meet state requirements. We’ll help you balance protection with affordability.

    Pennsylvania law requires specific minimum coverage levels for motorcycles, and we’ll ensure you meet or exceed those requirements. Beyond the basics, we can customize your policy with add-ons like accessory coverage for helmets and gear, or increased liability limits for extra protection.
