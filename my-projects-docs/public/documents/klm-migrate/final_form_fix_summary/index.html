    ✅ No white/gray bars on left and right (form will be full width) ✅ Single red asterisk for required fields (First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone) ✅ No asterisk on Comments field (it’s optional now) ✅ No double asterisks (removed “*” from field labels) ✅ All 5 fields visible from top to bottom ✅ Orange submit button at bottom
