    Apartment / Multi-Family Dwelling Coverage#

    Overview#

    Insurance for multi-family residential properties including apartments, duplexes, and larger complexes.

    Coverage Components#

    • Property coverage
    • General liability
    • Loss of rents
    • Equipment breakdown
    • Umbrella liability

    Target Market#

    • Individual property owners (2-20 units)
    • Small property management companies
    • Real estate investors

    Underwriting Factors#

    • Number of units
    • Age and condition
    • Safety features (sprinklers, alarms)
    • Tenant screening processes
    • Claims history

    Value-Add Services#

    • Annual property reviews
    • Risk management guidance
    • Certificate management for mortgagees
    • Loss control recommendations
