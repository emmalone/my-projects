    Business Owner Policy (BOP)#

    Overview#

    Packaged commercial insurance for small to medium-sized businesses.

    Standard Coverage#

    • Property insurance
    • General liability
    • Business interruption
    • Optional coverages (crime, inland marine, etc)

    Eligible Business Types#

    • Retail stores
    • Offices
    • Restaurants
    • Service businesses
    • Light manufacturing

    Pricing Strategy#

    BOPs are typically easier to quote and bind than custom commercial packages, making them good entry-level commercial products.

    Cross-Sell Opportunities#

    • Commercial auto
    • Workers compensation
    • Cyber liability
    • Employment practices liability
    • Professional liability

    Service Model#

    • Annual reviews
    • Certificate issuance
    • Claims advocacy
    • Risk management consulting
