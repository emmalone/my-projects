    Document Collection System#

    Overview#

    A structured system for identifying, organizing, and storing critical documents that families need during emergencies or transitions.

    Document Categories#

    Legal Documents#

    • Will
    • Trust documents
    • Power of attorney (financial)
    • Healthcare proxy / medical POA
    • Living will / advance directive
    • Guardianship designations
    • Divorce decrees
    • Prenuptial agreements

    Financial Documents#

    • Life insurance policies
    • Auto insurance policies
    • Home/property insurance
    • Disability insurance
    • Long-term care insurance
    • Health insurance cards
    • Bank account information
    • Investment account statements
    • Retirement account (401k, IRA) statements
    • Pension information
    • Social Security statements
    • Tax returns (last 3-7 years)
    • Property deeds
    • Vehicle titles
    • Mortgage documents
    • Loan agreements
    • Credit card list

    Personal Records#

    • Birth certificate
    • Marriage certificate
    • Passport
    • Social Security card
    • Military discharge papers (DD-214)
    • Citizenship/immigration papers

    Medical Information#

    • Medical history summary
    • Medication list
    • Physician contact information
    • Medical directives
    • Organ donation registration
    • HIPAA authorization forms

    Digital Assets#

    • Password list (encrypted)
    • Account list (email, social media, etc)
    • Digital asset inventory
    • Cryptocurrency keys/access
    • Cloud storage locations

    Business Documents (if applicable)#

    • Business formation documents
    • Operating agreements
    • Buy-sell agreements
    • Business succession plan
    • Key person insurance

    Implementation#

    Phase 1: Discovery#

    Client completes initial checklist identifying which documents exist and where they are located.

    Phase 2: Upload#

    Documents uploaded to Digital Life Vault with appropriate categorization.

    Phase 3: Organization#

    System creates a master index with location information for documents not stored digitally.

    Phase 4: Sharing#

    Critical documents shared with trusted family members or advisors with appropriate permissions.

    Phase 5: Maintenance#

    Annual review ensures documents are current and accessible.

    Features#

    • Checklist Progress: Visual indicator of completion
    • Location Tracking: Physical location of documents not uploaded
    • Expiration Alerts: Reminders when documents need updating
    • Gap Identification: Highlights missing critical documents
    • Templates: Sample documents and explanations
    • Guidance: Why each document matters and how to obtain

    Agent Value#

    Provides structure for client meetings and demonstrates thoroughness. Creates natural upsell opportunities (identify missing coverages, estate planning needs, etc).
